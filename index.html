Auto-escalation policy (enforced by the alert engine)

GREEN
Unacknowledged for 30 min → escalates to AMBER. Recipients: assigned doctor + support staff.
AMBER
Unacknowledged for 15 min → escalates to RED. Recipients: doctor + team medical + admin on duty.
RED
Unacknowledged for 3 min → auto-call cascade to admin mobiles. Recipients: high-alert team + CATS/BLS/ACLS + leadership.
BLACK
Immediate, no SLA. All channels + manual call cascade. Mass incident / multi-athlete / lost contact.
